Lion's Mane Guide

Species

Hericium erinaceus

Flavor

Delicate, slightly sweet—often compared to crab or lobster.

Texture

Tender, pull‑apart strands with a satisfying bite when seared.

Best Uses

Pan‑seared “steaks,” shredded tacos, ramen, creamy pastas, soups.

Typical Pack

Sold in ~½ lb increments; actual weight varies by harvest.

Harvest Window

Year‑round cultivation; fresh supply varies weekly—contact to confirm.

Storage & Shelf Life

  • Refrigerate unwashed in a paper bag; avoid sealed plastic.
  • Use fresh within 3–5 days for peak texture and flavor.
  • To dry: slice evenly and dehydrate until crisp; store airtight.
  • To rehydrate: soak 15–20 minutes in warm water/broth, then cook.

Safety & Disclaimers

  • Cook thoroughly for best flavor and digestibility.
  • Avoid if you have known mushroom allergies or sensitivities.
  • Cultivated species only; no foraging identification provided.
  • No medical claims—consult a professional for health questions.
