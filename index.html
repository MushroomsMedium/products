Lion’s Mane — Quick Facts

Species
Hericium erinaceus
Flavor
Delicate, slightly sweet; often compared to seafood (crab/lobster).
Texture
Tender yet meaty; strands pull apart.
Best Uses
Seared “steaks,” pan‑fried with butter, shredded for tacos, in soups.
Typical Pack Size
Commonly sold in ~½ lb increments; actual weight varies by harvest.

Storage, Shelf Life & Safety

Storage & Shelf Life (Lion’s Mane)

  • Refrigerate unwashed in a paper bag; avoid sealed plastic.
  • Use within 3–5 days for best texture and flavor.
  • To dry: slice into even pieces and dehydrate until crisp; store airtight.
  • To rehydrate: soak in warm water or broth 15–20 minutes, then cook.

Safety & Disclaimers

  • Cook thoroughly before eating for best flavor and digestibility.
